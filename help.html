Shipping Info

To help better serve our customers, we’ve started moving into our new automated fulfillment center and due to additional safety measures, we’re experiencing delays in shipping orders and processing returns. Your order might be delayed up to 5 days. Additionally, because we are still shipping from our old fulfillment center, your order might ship in TWO separate packages.

The health and safety of our customers and teams is our #1 priority and we appreciate your patience and understanding as we work to ship your order. If anything about your experience is not what you expected, please let us know at 1-123-345-4567 or contact customer service through our website: Contact us

International Shoppers: ☎ 1-123-456-7890

Orders are packed and shipped Monday-Friday only. Most orders are shipped within 24 hours from the order date. Orders placed on the weekend and select holidays are processed on the next business day.
